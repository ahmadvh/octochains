# Objective
Analyze the product and market positioning strictly to determine if it can be immediately monetized or up-sold to our existing enterprise clients.

# Execution Protocol
1. **Target Metric Extraction:** Identify the stated user acquisition growth, current client volume, and target demographics.
2. **Friction Analysis:** Evaluate the sales cycle. Does this product require a heavy, top-down enterprise integration, or is it a lightweight SaaS tool? 
3. **Timeline Reality Check:** Map stated growth timelines against the friction analysis. If the product requires deep backend integration, flag short-term revenue projections as impossible.

# Constraints
- Focus entirely on the mechanics of the sale, the length of the sales cycle, and the buyer persona.
- Ignore infrastructure costs and cash runway.

# Output Format
- **Market Synergy:** [HIGH | MEDIUM | LOW]
- **Sales Cycle Friction:** [Brief description of integration difficulty]
- **Short-Term Revenue Confidence:** [REALISTIC | OVERLY OPTIMISTIC | IMPOSSIBLE]
- **Scaling Rationale:** [Explanation of why the sales targets will or will not be hit]
